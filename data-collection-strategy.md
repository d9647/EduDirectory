# Tri-Valley Educational Data Collection Strategy

## Overview
This document outlines the strategy for collecting educational listings (tutoring, camps, internships, jobs) in the Tri-Valley area (Dublin, Pleasanton, Livermore, San Ramon).

## Phase 1: Quick Start (CSV Import)

### Priority Data Sources

#### 1. **Tutoring Providers**
**Target: 50-100 listings**

**Primary Sources:**
- **Yelp** - Search "tutoring" in Dublin, Pleasanton, Livermore
- **Google My Business** - Search "math tutor", "reading tutor", "test prep"
- **Local Business Directories** - Chamber of Commerce websites
- **School District Websites** - After-school enrichment programs

**Key Companies to Target:**
- Mathnasium (Dublin, Pleasanton)
- Kumon Centers (all cities)
- Sylvan Learning Centers
- Huntington Learning Centers
- Private tutors (via Yelp/Google)

#### 2. **Summer Camps**
**Target: 30-50 listings**

**Primary Sources:**
- **School District Websites** - Summer enrichment programs
- **City Recreation Departments** - Summer camps
- **YMCA/Boys & Girls Clubs** - Summer programs
- **Local Universities** - Pre-college programs

**Key Programs to Target:**
- Dublin Unified Summer Programs
- Pleasanton Unified Summer Camps
- Livermore Valley Joint Unified Programs
- UC Berkeley Summer Sessions
- Stanford Pre-Collegiate Studies

#### 3. **Internships**
**Target: 20-30 listings**

**Primary Sources:**
- **Lawrence Livermore National Laboratory** - Student programs
- **Sandia National Laboratories** - Internship programs
- **Local Government** - City internships
- **School Districts** - Education internships

#### 4. **Jobs**
**Target: 50-100 listings**

**Primary Sources:**
- **Retail Stores** - Safeway, Target, Walmart
- **Restaurants** - Fast food, casual dining
- **Recreation Centers** - YMCA, city pools
- **Libraries** - Part-time positions

## Phase 2: Data Collection Methods

### Method 1: Manual Research
**Tools Needed:**
- Google Sheets for data entry
- Yelp Business API (if available)
- Google Places API (limited free tier)

**Process:**
1. Search each source systematically
2. Extract business information
3. Standardize data format
4. Validate contact information

### Method 2: Web Scraping
**Target Websites:**
- Yelp (respect robots.txt)
- Google My Business listings
- Chamber of Commerce directories
- School district websites

**Legal Considerations:**
- Check robots.txt before scraping
- Respect rate limits
- Verify terms of service
- Consider reaching out to businesses directly

### Method 3: Direct Outreach
**Strategy:**
- Contact businesses directly via phone/email
- Offer free listing on your platform
- Provide incentives for early adoption
- Build relationships with local business owners

## Phase 3: Data Validation & Enhancement

### Quality Control Process
1. **Verify Contact Information**
   - Call phone numbers
   - Check website URLs
   - Validate email addresses

2. **Standardize Data**
   - Consistent address formatting
   - Standardized categories
   - Uniform pricing formats

3. **Enhance Listings**
   - Add photos (from business websites)
   - Write compelling descriptions
   - Categorize properly
